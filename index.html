WHAT IS INOUT

Mathematically,
InOut = ( 300 makers + 30 hours ) * ( caffeine + ideas ) / 42.0

InOut is one of India’s largest student run hackathon. Twice a year the best developers, designers and hardware enthusiasts from across India gather at NIT Surat to innovate and build on an idea. This third edition of InOut will span for 30 hours over the course of the second weekend of August. Food and swags are on us.

All in all InOut is a great place to make both amazing friends and something that makes a difference.

InOut 2.0 was blast. Checkout all the fun you missed :).

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What if I don't have a team or an idea?

If you don’t have a team now, don’t stress! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with.

How much will it cost?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. InOut is free for all accepted hackers. We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of swag, meals, drinks, and snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding.

When does registration open?

Registration will open on Sept. 15th 12:00am IST and will close on Oct. 18th 23:59pm IST. If you are accepted, you’ll have five days to confirm your attendance. Do Sign up for our updates!

What's the application process like?

We're looking for creative and innovative people with a set of skills that can contribute to a project. We generally prefer people that have proven their abilities in previous projects, evident through Github links, project descriptions, or participation/awards in other hackathons.
