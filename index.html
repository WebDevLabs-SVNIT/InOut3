WHAT IS INOUT

Mathematically,
InOut = ( 300 makers + 30 hours ) * ( caffeine + ideas ) / 42.0

InOut is one of Indiaâ€™s largest student run hackathon. Twice a year the best developers, designers and hardware enthusiasts from across India gather at NIT Surat to innovate and build on an idea.
