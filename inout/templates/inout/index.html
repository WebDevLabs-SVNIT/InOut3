WHAT IS INOUT?

Mathematically,
InOut = ( 500 makers + 30 hours ) * ( caffeine + ideas ) / 42.0


InOut is one of India’s largest student run hackathon. Twice a year the best developers, designers and hardware enthusiasts from across India gather at NIT Surat to innovate and build on an idea. This third edition of InOut will span for 30 hours over the course of the second weekend of August.


Whether you’re writing your first lines of HTML, building a VR app or a hoverboard. InOut is just the place for you. We will have great workshops and mentors to help make your idea happen. You will be able to show your skills without getting worried about any mortal needs. We will take care of everything.


All in all InOut is a great place to make both amazing friends and something that makes a difference.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What if I don't have a team or an idea?

If you don’t have a team now, don’t stress! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with.

How much will it cost?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. InOut is free for all accepted hackers. We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of swag, meals, drinks, and snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding.

What's the application process like?

We're looking for creative and innovative people with a set of skills that can contribute to a project. We generally prefer people that have proven their abilities in previous projects, evident through GitHub links, project descriptions, or participation/awards in other hackathons.

How big can a team be?

This hackathon is strictly a team competition. No lone crusaders please. Max team size : 3 members.

Do you give travel reimbursements?

We will provide travel reimbursements post review by our organization committee. Keep your eyes peeled on this FAQ answer for more details!

What about accommodation?

We’ve made special arrangements for subsidized accommodation in case you're arriving a day before the event.

When does registration open?

Registration will open on June 12th 10:00pm IST and will close on July 12th 23:59pm IST. If you are accepted, you’ll have five days to confirm your attendance.

Who can attend?

All undergraduate, graduate, and high school students are welcome. If you are under 18, we’ll need a parental consent form.

I'm not a student, so how can I get involved?

Unfortunately, we can't allow you to participate in the event or be on site during hacking. If you're looking to be a mentor (i.e. someone with technical skills who'd be willing to help out participants during the weekend), you should reach out to us at mentor@hackinout.co

Once I get accepted, what do I need to bring?

Your valid student ID, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want (we'll be providing mattresses) and nerdy t-shirt!

What do I get by attending InOut?

Pretty much everything from socializing with fellow hacker community to internships/job offers from coolest tech companies. And yeah awesome prizes (worth ₹ 10,00,000) too!

What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

Please email us at mail@hackinout.co if you have any concerns at all! We’re friendly, and we’d love to help you out!
